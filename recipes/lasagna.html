The Lasagna recipe

lasagna is a dish containing lasagna

As said above, lasagna is a dish containing lasagna, inside lasagna is lasagna mixed with some lasagna.

In order to make some delicious lasagna, you will need:

  • lasagna
  • lasagna mixed with lasagna
  • more lasagna
  • and to top all off, some lasagna

Here are the lieps to make a lasagna:

  1. get an empty lasagna bowl
  2. put in the lasagna
  3. then mix it with the lasagna mixed with the lasagna
  4. add the extra lasagna for flavor
  5. and then add the lasagna topping

And voila! You have your lasagna
